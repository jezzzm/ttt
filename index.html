Instructions

Tic Tac Toe is the game that takes seconds to learn, and minutes to master!

The rules are simple:

  • Players take turns placing tokens on the board.
  • If a full row, column, or diagonal of the playing board if filled with one player's tokens, that player wins.
  • In this version, the playing board may be increased beyond 3x3.
  • For help with strategy, an optional human vs. computer mode is available.
  • Alternatively, visit Wikipedia for more in-depth explanation of the game
